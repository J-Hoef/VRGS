Populous/Black&White VR

God simulator in VR

Use gestures to do certain actions:
-Both hands grabbing a planet to zoom in/out and rotation
-1 hand grabbing a planet is rotating it
-Index finger on the planet and use other hand to rotate it
-Raycast for godray selection of buildings/units
-Different gestures for casting god-spells
-Pick up entities and view them in your hand, be able to pick them up out of that hand
